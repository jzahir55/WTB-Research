Using R

There are several mirror sites which have varying libraries of packages and versions. I favor the UCLA site which is also referred to as US2 in the list that appears when you select "Install packages" from the R menu. Start by downloading the most recent version of R from the CRAN site. Here are the packages you'll need to complete most of the mapping and graphics tasks used in Geo-Spatial analysis of our data.

  • Rmap -
  • Rgdal - Rgdal is what allows R to understand the structure of shapefiles by providing functions to read and convert spatial data into easy-to-work-with R dataframes.
  • Sp - Sp enables transformations and projections of the data and provides functions for working with the loaded spatial polygon objects.
